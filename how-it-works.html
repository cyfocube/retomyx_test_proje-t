Why a meet & greet matters

Your pet's comfort comes first — always

A free 20-minute meet & greet lets your sitter learn your pet's quirks (the squeaky toy, the 4pm zoomies, the strict dinner schedule) and lets you see how they interact in person. If it's not a fit, no problem — message another sitter, no fees, no pressure.
